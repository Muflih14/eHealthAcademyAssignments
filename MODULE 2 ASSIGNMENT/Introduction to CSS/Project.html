A Trip to Africa: Nigeria

Introduction

Nigeria is a country located in the Western Part of Africa. It consists of 36 states, with the Federal Capital being Abuja.
Nigeria is a country made up of several tribes, with Hausa, Yoruba and Igbo being the predominanat ones. Its people
practice the religion of Islam, Christianity and Traditional religion. Two major rivers found in Nigeria are the
River Niger and River Benue.

A Brief History and Description

The country happens to be one of the many countries colonized in Africa by the British, and happened to gain its independence
in year 1960. After that, it has witnessed several ruling regimes, both military and democratic rules. Agriculture,
commerce and tourism are occupations that thrive in country. Its agricultural sector produces various crops, including edible and
cash crops. Fishing, livestock and poultry farming are also important aspects of its agricultural sector.
Nigeria witnesses a lot of festivals from different communities, tribes and religion all year round. Among these are the Argungu
festival, Durbar festival, Eyo festival and Islamic eids and Christian Christmas and easter festivities. Interesting
places to visit in Nigeria are:
  • Badagry Slave Trade Site
  • Yankari Game Reserve
  • Obudu Cattle Ranch
  • Kianji Dam

Other interesting places to visit in Nigeria are:
  1. Gashaka Gumti resort
  2. Sarangetti game reserve
  3. River Niger and Benue
