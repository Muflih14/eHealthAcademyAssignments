Meatpie Recipe

Meal Ingredients Quantity
Meatpie Flour
Beef
Potatoes
carrots
Baking powder
Seasoning
Salt
 2cups 1/2kg
2 large size
2 sticks
1/2tbsp
2 cubes
1 table spoon

Detailed List

  1. Prepare the meatpie dough
  2. Prepare the meatpie filling

Procedure

Prepare the meatpie filling by boiling the beef for 45 minutes wafter washing
properly. Add 1/2 table spoon of salt and a seasoning cube to taste. Peel the
potatoes and carrots, dice, and add the boiling beeef when tender. Allow to simmer for
amother 10 minutes before removing the pot from the fire. Sieve the contents from the
broth to avoid further cooking. After that, pick the beef chunks and shred with a meat
mincer. Add all three ingredients in bowl and keep aside.

Prepare the dough by adding a pinch of salt and a 1/2 tsp of baking powder. Add a little
water and make a nice consistent dough. Divide into small balls and flatten each ball with
a rolling pin. Use a meatpie shaper to cut out the shapes and add a little of the filling
set aside in each round dough. Fold the dough with the filling inside and flatten the edges
to ensure it doesnt open while baking. Place in an oven to bake for 20 minutes.

Detailed List

  1. Prepare the meat filling
  2. Prepare the vegetable filling

Procedure

Wash the meat properly and place in a pot with suffficient water. Add 1 seasoning
cube and 1/4tbs of salt. Allow to boil until tender.

Wash the tomatoes and cabbage with vinegar or salty water. Slice the cabbage into thin
shreds and dice the tomatoes into little bits. Dice the boiled meat into bits and mix all
three together to get a nice mix. Place a little of the result in between two slices of
bread each and your. Slice each combo to form diagonal shapes and your sandiwch is ready
to eat.
